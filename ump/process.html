Process

Our quartz sand offers numerous advantages, including its use as a raw material for glass. Our quartz sand particles are purified to a purity exceeding 99.5% and are ready for use. Our quartz sand is of the highest quality, suitable for use in a variety of high- and low-value products, including high-quality glass for bulletproof automotive windows, solar panels, and tempered glass for smartphones.
